## 强调句

It is ... that ...

I want to go to America this summer. => It is America that I want to go to this summer.

不能强调动词 / 形容词

## 倒装句

主谓倒装
1. 全部倒装

谓语动词全在主语前

Here *comes* the bus.

2. 部分倒装

Only on one occasion *have* I *felt* frightened.
